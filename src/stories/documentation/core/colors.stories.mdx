### Principles

<ul>
  <li>
    <b>Hierarchical</b>
    <p>
      Color indicates which elements are interactive, how they relate to other
      elements, and their level of prominence. Important elements should stand
      out the most.
    </p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Legible</b>
    <p>
      Text and important elements, like icons, should meet legibility standards
      when appearing on colored backgrounds.
    </p>
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Expressive</b>
    <p>
      Show brand colors at memorable moments that reinforce your brandâ€™s style.
    </p>
  </li>
</ul>
